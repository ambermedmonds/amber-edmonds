UX/UI Case Study

WashU Club Discovery Platform

This project focused on researching ways to help WashU students discover and join campus organizations, while making it easier for club leaders to recruit and onboard new members.

When

Feb-Apr 2025

Role

UX Research + Product Design

Tools

Figma, FigJam

Problem

Club discovery is fragmented across word-of-mouth, social media, flyers, and an online platform called WUGO. Info is often outdated or incomplete, making it hard for students to compare clubs and take next steps.

Process
  • Surveys and interviews to understand behavior patterns
  • Affinity mapping to synthesize pain points + opportunities
  • Persona and journey mapping for students and leaders
  • Prototype development and usability tests
Key Insights
  • Students want a single, easy-to-use hub for all club info
  • Students want clearer expectations before committing
  • Students want interest-based filters to find relevant clubs
Outcome

This concept centralizes club info, personalizes discovery, and streamlines onboarding workflows to improve how students find clubs and how leaders engage new members.
